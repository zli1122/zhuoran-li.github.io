## Brief Biography

Hi, my name is Zhuoran Li. I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Arizona.  
My research focuses on cybersecurity, cryptography, and hardware security, with an emphasis on hardware–algorithm co-design for secure and efficient systems.

## Education

- Ph.D. in Computer Science, Old Dominion University, USA, 2023 (advised by [Dr. Danella Zhao](https://ece.engineering.arizona.edu/faculty-staff/faculty/danella-zhao))  
- B.S. in Mechanical Engineering, Southwest Jiaotong University, China, 2014
